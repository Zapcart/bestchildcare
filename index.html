Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before donating.

Is my donation safe?

Yes. All payments are processed through RBI-compliant, bank-grade encrypted gateways. Your financial data is never stored on our servers. We use the same security standards as leading banks.

Where does the money go?

Every rupee goes directly to the campaign you choose. We publish utilization reports with receipts, photos, and field updates so you can see exactly how your donation was used.

Will I get updates?

Absolutely. Once you donate, you receive regular impact updates via email — including progress photos, milestone reports, and thank-you messages from the beneficiaries.

Can I donate products instead of money?

Yes! Our Donate Products section lets you send verified kits (groceries, hygiene, school supplies, meals) directly to the programs that need them.

How are campaigns verified?

Each campaign undergoes a multi-step verification: identity checks, document validation, field visits, and ongoing monitoring. We share all proof in our Documents section.
